Europoean 4 in 1 Chainmail

This is the most basic of chainmail weaves and is the easiest to execute. Anywhere in the weave, any one ring will have 4 adjecent rings enclosed in it, hence the name "4 in 1".

Materials

Only three things are required for this tutorial:

  • Pre-cut metal jump rings ( 16g 5/16" diameter rings are good place to start)
  • Two good pliers
  • Patience

Instructions

Step 1

*Note: gold indicates new rings and silver indicates old.

Close 4 rings and open 1. Loop all 4 onto the open ring, close it and align the rings as shown.

Step 2

Take an open ring and loop it over and under the two ending rings, then place two closed rings into the open ring and close it.

Step 3

Repeat Step 2 until desired length of chain.

Step 4

To continue as a sheet, take 1 open ring and loop it under the bottom two rings at the start of the weave. Place 2 closed rings, close it and align the rings as shown.

Step 5

Loop one open ring over the last ring of the new row and under the next two rings of the last row. Place 1 closed ring on and close the open one.

Step 6

Continue Step 5 for the remaining length of chain. Then repeat Steps 4 through 5 until desired sheet size is achieved.
