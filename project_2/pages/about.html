About Me

Chainmail is just one of several of my small hobbies. I started delving into weaving chainmail a few years ago and loved the end result of slowing piecing together a piece of mail or jewelry, one ring at a time. Although chainmail was intially used as armor, it's incredible how much practical stuff can be made with it; such as coasters, jewelry, hackey sacks, protective gear and so on. Weaving is almost theuaroputic and the possiblities with experimentation are endless as new weaves are invented every day!
